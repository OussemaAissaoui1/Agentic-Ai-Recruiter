AI Recruiter

One platform, three stages. Pre-screen CVs against a JD, run a multimodal interview with an AI recruiter avatar, and stream behavioral analysis from the candidate's webcam in real time.

Stage 1 — pre-interview

CV Screening

Upload a JD and a batch of CVs. The matching agent ranks every candidate (TAPJFNN + GNN), extracts hiring signals via LLM, and runs a constraint-aware GA shortlist.

 Stage 2 — live interview

AI Interview

Talk to Alex, the AI recruiter (Llama-3.1-8B + scorer + refiner + TTS). The vision agent records the candidate's webcam in parallel and streams behavioral signals back on top of the chat.

 Stage 3 — analytics

Behavioral Dashboard

Real-time dashboard for the vision agent's 4-D profile: cognitive load, emotional arousal, engagement, confidence — plus PDF/JSON reports per interview session.
API: GET /api/health · GET /.well-known/agents/<name> · /docs
